oleumer

oleumer is a model for early oil spill detection in satelliate imagery using Deep learning through a Deep CNN architecture. This model could be applied as an extension to services such as Google Earth Engine or NASA's public satelliate imagery intiatives as well. This way the model is monitoring real time images of bodies of water to detect oil spills before they become disastrous.
